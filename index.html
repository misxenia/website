About Me

I am a PhD student in the Computational Statistics and Machine Learning group, at the Department of Statistics of the University of Oxford working with Francois Caron and Yee Whye Teh. I am a student in the Center for Doctoral Training in modern statistical science. Previously, I completed a BSc in Mathematics at Imperial College London and a master in Mathematical Statistics at the University of Cambridge.
